Why Pickup

Built for players
who just want to play.

No season commitments, no team tryouts. Just open a map, find a game, and show up.

📍
Find games on a map
Every game pinned on a live map. See exactly where it's happening, how far away, and how many spots are left.
⚡
Join in one tap
No forms, no approval. See a game you like — tap Join. The organizer and players are notified instantly.
📣
Post your own game
Set a time, place, and sport. Pickup broadcasts it to nearby players. Fill your spots in minutes.
💬
Message players
Chat with players before the game — coordinate meetups, confirm spots, or set up a rematch.
🏆
Track your wins
Log game results, build your record, and see how your stats stack up across every sport you play.
🎬
Share your moments
Drop clips and highlights from your best games. React, follow, and build your player reputation.

How it works

From install to game
in under 2 minutes.

1
Download the app and set your sports
Pick the sports you play. Pickup immediately shows you every open game nearby, sorted by distance and start time.
2
Browse the live map
See pins for every game happening near you right now — tap any pin for details, player list, and skill level.
3
Join a game (or post your own)
Tap Join and you're in. Or tap the + button to post a new game — tell the community what you're running.
4
Show up and play
Get a push notification 15 minutes before tip-off. Show up, run your game, and come back to log the result.
